Detailed Timeline

This section provides a detailed timeline of the strange anomalies discovered throughout the Counter-Strike: Source maps.

Event 1 - Dust2 Error: In early 2023, players began noticing strange errors on the famous "de_dust2" map. Triggers that should have activated certain events were removed, causing players to wander into hidden areas that were not part of the normal map design.

Event 2 - Italy Glitch: Around the same time, "cs_italy" saw unexplained glitches, with players reporting that enemies appeared in spots they couldn't normally access. This phenomenon baffled many players.

Event 3 - Nuke Shadows: A bizarre event occurred on "de_nuke" when players started noticing shadows moving without any light source. The anomaly was captured on multiple streams.

Event 4 - Office Item Spawns: On "cs_office," rare items began spawning at random locations, with no clear trigger, leading players to investigate whether the map had been tampered with.
