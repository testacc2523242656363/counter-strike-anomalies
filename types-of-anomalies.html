Types of Anomalies

Here are some of the most common types of anomalies reported in Counter-Strike: Source:

  • Glitching Areas: Hidden areas on the map that players can unintentionally access, often due to improperly placed triggers or invisible walls.
  • Strange Entity Behavior: Unexpected behaviors from game entities, like NPCs or objects, appearing or acting in ways they shouldn't.
  • Invisible Walls: Players find themselves unable to pass through certain areas without any visible barriers, often resulting from map errors.
  • Audio Bugs: Certain sounds or audio cues play unexpectedly, often in areas that have no apparent reason for their presence.
  • Texture Anomalies: Unusual or incorrect textures appearing in game, often causing a distorted visual experience.
